Money is moving before the decision is made.

Funds get released. Enrollments get confirmed. Audits happen later. By then, the ineligible participant is already in the system and the funding is already at risk.

CACS moves the decision to the front. Before enrollment. Before disbursement. Before it is too late to act on it.

How it works.

1. Verify inputs. Identity and eligibility are evaluated against your program's funding rules before the decision runs.

2. Issue decision. The engine produces one output — ALLOW or BLOCK. Versioned, timestamped, and cited to the applicable funding rule.

3. Enforce decision. ALLOW opens the funding gate. BLOCK holds it. No funds move until the decision clears.
